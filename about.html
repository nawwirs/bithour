About Us.

Bithour Production is a production house and creative studio based in Jakarta. We produce films, Web Series, TV Commercials, Video Profiles, 2D Animations, Documentary Videos, and Digital Magazines. Our vision is to be the number one production house with high standards of quality and consistency. Our mission is to collaborate with brands and companies to create and share stories through the screen. We will help you to make your concepts come true with an affordable price and creative visualization.
